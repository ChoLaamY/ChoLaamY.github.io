Research Projects

Effect of Perceived Emotional Similarity within Teams on Organizational Outcomes

In this analytical project I examined whether increased perceived emotional similarity between co-workers working in teams will have a positive impact on organizational outcomes (e.g. worker productivity and job satisfaction) and predict shared reality. I built a mixed model to answer this question, accounting for greater perceived emotional similarity of individuals within teams compared to between teams.
